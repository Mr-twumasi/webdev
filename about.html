About Us

Word Liberty Chapel International is a growing family of believers committed to worshipping God, spreading the Gospel, and building lives that shine with purpose and love.

Our Mission Statement

Transforming society through Christ. Through our programs, we strive to make the lives of our people better than before they came to us, redirecting their perception and behavior in conformity with God's word. We believe that which is based on the word of God will transform the lives of people in communities, workplaces, schools, and beyond.

Our History

Word Liberty Chapel International was founded with a vision to create a community where faith, fellowship, and service come together to transform lives. What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant church family committed to spreading God's love and truth.

Over the years, we have witnessed countless lives changed through the power of God's Word, meaningful relationships built, and communities strengthened through our various outreach programs and ministries.

Our Logo (Acts 2:41-42)

Acts 2:41-42 - "So those who received his word were baptized, and there were added that day about three thousand souls. And they devoted themselves to the apostles' teaching and the fellowship, to the breaking of bread and the prayers."

Our logo represents the foundation of our church: devotion to God's Word, fellowship among believers, breaking of bread (communion), and prayer. The open Bible symbolizes our commitment to scriptural teaching, while the flame represents the Holy Spirit's presence in our midst.

Our Message (Jeremiah 31:17)

"There is hope for your future, declares the Lord, and your children shall come back to their own country."

Jeremiah 31:17 (ESV)

This powerful message of hope and restoration forms the cornerstone of our ministry. We believe that God has a plan and purpose for every life, and through Christ, there is always hope for a better future. Our message emphasizes spiritual restoration, family healing, and community transformation through faith in Jesus Christ.

Our Philosophy

At Word Liberty Chapel International, our philosophy is rooted in the belief that every person has inherent worth and potential that can only be fully realized through a relationship with Jesus Christ. We are committed to:

  • Biblical Teaching: All our programs and activities are grounded in the unchanging truth of God's Word.
  • Authentic Relationships: We foster genuine connections where people can grow spiritually and emotionally.
  • Community Impact: We believe the church should be a force for positive change in society.
  • Spiritual Growth: We provide opportunities for believers to mature in their faith and discover their calling.
  • Service and Outreach: We actively engage in meeting needs and sharing God's love with our community.

Statement Of Faith

We Believe:

  • In the one true God who eternally exists in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • In the divine inspiration and authority of the Holy Scriptures.
  • In the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, miracles, death, resurrection, ascension, and future return.
  • In the salvation of sinners by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • In the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who indwells and empowers believers for godly living and service.
  • In the spiritual unity of all believers in Christ.
  • In the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; the saved unto eternal life and the lost unto eternal separation from God.

Vision Of Word Liberty Chapel International (Acts 2:42)

"And they devoted themselves to the apostles' teaching and the fellowship, to the breaking of bread and the prayers."

Acts 2:42 (ESV)

Our vision is to be a church that embodies the early church model described in Acts 2:42. We strive to be a community where:

  • Apostles' Teaching: Sound biblical instruction forms the foundation of all we do.
  • Fellowship: Genuine relationships and community support are central to our church life.
  • Breaking of Bread: Regular communion and shared meals strengthen our bonds.
  • Prayers: Corporate and individual prayer are essential to our spiritual vitality.

Through this vision, we aim to impact generations with the transformative power of God's love and truth.
